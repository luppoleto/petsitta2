Meet The Petsitta

Caring for pets has always been an important part of my life. I count myself fortunate to have grown up amongst a gang of fuzzy friends and I’ve proudly continued that tradition in my own home.

As a long time pet owner, I’ve accumulated a wealth of knowledge. Each new companion has offered a fresh perspective, a sprinkling of frustration and – of course - immense joy.

To accompany this experience, I have spent the last 7 years actively pursuing a more formal education; focusing on animal training and advanced care. I am eager to share and apply all that I’ve learned!

I cover clients from the Farmington Valley to Manchester and all points in between! If I can't get there, I may be able to recommend someone in your region.
